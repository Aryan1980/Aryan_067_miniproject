Projects Overview

These are the projects that I made with the help of my society in college these projects solve very general problems using technology.

Project 1 : AI Video Summarization and Quiz Generation

A tool that transcribes educational videos to text using speech recognition and then it summarizes the content and generates quiz questions on it's own.
Technologies include Large Language Models (LLMs) and speech-to-text models like Whisper.
Helps automate learning material creation.

Project 2 : Stock Price Prediction

A stock predictor uses advanced algorithms, historical data, and other indicators to forecast the future value and movements of stocks or other financial instruments.
These predictions are not guaranteed though it is less accurate due to the market's inherent complexity and unpredictability.

Project 3 : Credit Card Fraud Detection

A credit card fraud predictor is an advanced system, primarily powered by artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML), used by financial institutions to analyze transaction data in real-time and identify suspicious activities that may indicate fraud.
Instead of relying solely on static, predefined rules, modern fraud predictors leverage vast amounts of historical transaction data to learn and adapt to emerging fraud patterns.
